Privacy & Data Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy & Data Policy applies to A.S. Anti-Scam App (the “App”), developed and operated by A.S. Cyber Intelligence Federal Inc. (“we”, “us”, “our”).

Official website: ascyber.ca
Contact email: andaaida@ascyber.ca

1) Summary

  • The App is designed to help users assess QR content and links using offline checks.
  • We do not create accounts and do not require login.
  • We do not collect or store personal data from the App by default.
  • When you choose to open a link, the App uses your device’s browser (via the system) to open it.

2) Data We Collect

We do not collect personal information such as name, email, phone number, contacts, precise location, or photos through the App.

The App processes the text you paste or scan (e.g., a URL or QR payload) locally on your device to display a risk level.

We do not transmit your scanned/pasted content to our servers.

3) How the App Works (Offline Processing)

The App uses local rules (heuristics) to evaluate the input. Examples may include:

  • whether the link uses HTTP vs HTTPS
  • unusual domain patterns
  • presence of suspicious keywords

This analysis is informational only and may not detect every scam. Always verify the sender and domain.

4) Permissions

Camera (mobile only): used to scan QR codes.

On desktop platforms (Windows/Mac/Linux), camera scanning may be disabled, and you can paste text instead.

5) Third-Party Services

The App may use the device’s system capability to open links (for example, using a browser). When you open a link, you are subject to the privacy policies of the website you visit.

No advertising SDKs or analytics SDKs are required for the App to function.

6) Data Retention

We do not store scanned/pasted content on our servers.

Any content you paste or scan is processed on-device. If your device stores clipboard history or browser history, that is controlled by your device and browser settings.

7) Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8) Security

We aim to keep the App minimal and offline-first to reduce data exposure. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

9) Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
